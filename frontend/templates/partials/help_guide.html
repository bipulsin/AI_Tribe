AI Tribe

Submit up to 10 photos and an optional short video of vehicle damage. Watch the assessment pipeline run, then review a parts-priced survey estimate.

  • Vehicle Forensics Authenticity and reuse checks on submitted media before pricing starts.
  • Sensor Consistency Within-claim noise-residual check — flags photos that look like a different source, not full PRNU device attribution.
  • VMMR Make/model recognition with confidence tiers — low-confidence guesses need surveyor confirmation.
  • Multi-Image Cross-Vehicle Consistency Compares images within a claim so mixed-vehicle submissions surface as risk signals.
  • Organised Fraud Graph Intelligence Network of claimants, surveyors, and garages linked by shared claims — flags high-degree nodes and recurring garage–surveyor pairs.
  • Parts-Grounded Estimation Maps damage to catalogue parts and prices; public-source prices are indicative only.
